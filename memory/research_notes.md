# Detailed Research Notes

## Key Statistics to Remember

### SDR Economics
- $60K SDR → $22,200/yr wasted on research alone
- 29 hours avg lead response time, 63% never responded to
- 81% of teams use AI, but only 1% have mature deployments
- 79% of automation deployments fail to hit ROI targets

### CRM Data
- 91% annual decay rate; 9% accuracy after 12 months
- 37% of staff fabricate data due to friction
- $3.1T annual cost of poor data quality in US
- Human error rate: 1% ideal, 4% without verification (100x worse than automated)

### Pipeline & Forecasting
- 50-70% forecast reliability is the norm
- $15M avg annual loss due to pipeline data quality
- Intelligence tools inherit all underlying data errors

### Outreach
- B2B cold email reply rates: 1-5% (down from 7% two years ago)
- 100-120 sales emails/week per decision maker
- Signal-based personalization → 18% response rate (5.2x average)
- 5 min of account research → 3-5x higher reply rates

### Lead Handoff
- $1T+ annual cost of sales-marketing misalignment
- 5 min response → 100x more likely to convert vs 1 hour
- 79% of marketing leads never convert

### Coaching
- Weekly coaching → 76% quota attainment
- Quarterly coaching → 47% quota attainment
- 2+ hrs/week coaching → win rates jump 43% to 56%
- 12.1 avg direct reports per manager (up from 10.9 in 2024)

### Multi-Threading
- Single-threaded: 5% win rate
- Multi-threaded: 25-35% win rate
- 3+ departments: 44% win rate vs 28% single-department
- Closes 53-78 days faster
- 18-month avg buyer tenure

### Tool Stack
- 8-10 tools daily per rep
- True cost: 2-2.5x license fees
- Consolidation (14→6 tools): +22% revenue/rep, -$210K/yr savings
- 87 avg software tools in org, 23% impact revenue

### Territory
- 36% effectiveness rate
- Weighted TAM = Fit × Revenue Potential is the modern approach

### Intent Data
- Below 20% accuracy (MIT study)
- $4.5B market, 24% report strong ROI
- 94% pricing page visitors not sales-ready

### Contract/Legal
- 73 days avg delay ($500K+ deals)
- 94 days avg delay ($2M+ deals)
- 23% of "commit" deals collapse in legal
- Contract intelligence → 58% faster legal passage

### Competitive Intelligence
- Stale in 30 days, updated quarterly (10+ week lag)
- 8-12 hrs/month per rep on manual research
- $195K-$292.5K annual cost for 50-person team
- AI coaching → 21% win rate improvement

### Post-Sale Expansion
- 5x more spent on acquisition vs expansion
- Expansion: 50-70% success rate, 80-90% cheaper, 2-3x faster
- <3% upsell acceptance rate common
- NRR 110%+ → premium valuations

### Demo No-Shows (Session 2)
- 6.5% average no-show rate, but 23-47% at 8+ days out
- Same-day demos: 6.9% no-show rate (3.3-6.8x better)
- $730 per no-show in burned marketing spend
- $1.8M-$3.6M annual pipeline leak per 10-person SDR team
- 30-min slots: 12% higher attendance than 60-min
- SMB (1-10 employees): 50% no-show; Enterprise (1,000+): 10%
- 88% of no-shows from scheduling conflicts/priority shifts, not forgetfulness

### Sales Onboarding & Ramp (Session 2)
- SDR ramp: 3.2 months; Mid-market AE: 6 months; Enterprise AE: 9+ months
- Best-in-class: 2 months to full productivity
- $115K-$195K total cost per SDR departure
- 20% quit within 90 days; 44% within 12 months
- 14-18 month average SDR tenure
- 88% of companies have subpar onboarding
- Structured onboarding → 82% longer retention
- AI tools reduce ramp by 30%, reps 3.7x more likely to hit quota

### Content Enablement (Session 2)
- $2.3M annual loss from underutilized content (G2)
- Content scattered across Drive, SharePoint, CMS
- Reps ask "Does anyone have that case study?" in Slack
- Problem is delivery (last mile), not content quality
- Search introduces friction in high-pressure sales moments

### Commission Errors (Session 2)
- 3-8% error rates on manual commission processes
- $300K-$800K miscalculations on $10M budget
- 22% of reps have 1+ dispute per year
- 42% of sellers quit after compensation dispute
- 9% quit specifically over commission errors
- ~36 hours per payout period operational time
- Only 50% provide real-time earnings visibility

### Sales-to-CS Handoff (Session 2)
- 62% of CS leaders lack real-time onboarding visibility
- 60%+ handoffs missing critical qualitative context
- 57% report onboarding friction impacts revenue
- Misaligned incentives: sales → closure, CS → retention
- Customers forced to repeat themselves

### Email Deliverability (Session 2)
- Gmail/Yahoo require DMARC, SPF, DKIM for 5,000+ daily senders
- Non-compliance = direct rejection (not just spam folder)
- 0.1% spam complaint threshold (extremely tight for cold outreach)
- Fresh domains blacklisted for up to 90 days
- Multiple tools without consistent auth = systemic failures

### Quota Setting (Session 2)
- 87% of leaders have no quota-setting methodology
- "Peanut butter spread" = flat % increase across all reps
- 76.6% of sellers missed quota in 2025
- 67% of reps don't expect to meet quota
- Misaligned quotas → disengagement, gaming, turnover

### Pricing/Discounting (Session 2)
- 15-30% margin erosion from poor pricing discipline
- Discounts creep from 12% to 22% in 6 months without win rate gains
- Buyers institutionalize expectations of concessions
- 1% improvement in realized price has outsized earnings impact
- Dual reality: list prices vs. actual negotiated prices

## Cross-Cutting Themes
1. **Data quality is the root cause of many downstream problems** — CRM decay feeds into bad forecasts, bad territories, bad intent signals
2. **Time poverty is universal** — SDRs, managers, and CS teams all lack time for their highest-value activities
3. **AI adoption is early and failing** — 81% use AI but 1% mature; 79% of automation fails ROI targets
4. **Personalization vs Scale is the central tension** — quality drops as volume increases across every channel
5. **Handoff points are revenue leaks** — marketing→sales, sales→legal, sales→CS all lose context and speed
6. **Invisible costs compound silently** — commission errors, discount creep, no-show pipeline leaks, and content waste accumulate without visible metrics
7. **Governance gaps enable degradation** — without guardrails on pricing, quotas, and commission calculations, individual rep decisions erode organizational outcomes
8. **Onboarding is a retention lever** — ramp time directly correlates with tenure; 88% of companies underinvest in structured onboarding
