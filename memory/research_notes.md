# Detailed Research Notes

## Key Statistics to Remember

### SDR Economics
- $60K SDR → $22,200/yr wasted on research alone
- 29 hours avg lead response time, 63% never responded to
- 81% of teams use AI, but only 1% have mature deployments
- 79% of automation deployments fail to hit ROI targets

### CRM Data
- 91% annual decay rate; 9% accuracy after 12 months
- 37% of staff fabricate data due to friction
- $3.1T annual cost of poor data quality in US
- Human error rate: 1% ideal, 4% without verification (100x worse than automated)

### Pipeline & Forecasting
- 50-70% forecast reliability is the norm
- $15M avg annual loss due to pipeline data quality
- Intelligence tools inherit all underlying data errors

### Outreach
- B2B cold email reply rates: 1-5% (down from 7% two years ago)
- 100-120 sales emails/week per decision maker
- Signal-based personalization → 18% response rate (5.2x average)
- 5 min of account research → 3-5x higher reply rates

### Lead Handoff
- $1T+ annual cost of sales-marketing misalignment
- 5 min response → 100x more likely to convert vs 1 hour
- 79% of marketing leads never convert

### Coaching
- Weekly coaching → 76% quota attainment
- Quarterly coaching → 47% quota attainment
- 2+ hrs/week coaching → win rates jump 43% to 56%
- 12.1 avg direct reports per manager (up from 10.9 in 2024)

### Multi-Threading
- Single-threaded: 5% win rate
- Multi-threaded: 25-35% win rate
- 3+ departments: 44% win rate vs 28% single-department
- Closes 53-78 days faster
- 18-month avg buyer tenure

### Tool Stack
- 8-10 tools daily per rep
- True cost: 2-2.5x license fees
- Consolidation (14→6 tools): +22% revenue/rep, -$210K/yr savings
- 87 avg software tools in org, 23% impact revenue

### Territory
- 36% effectiveness rate
- Weighted TAM = Fit × Revenue Potential is the modern approach

### Intent Data
- Below 20% accuracy (MIT study)
- $4.5B market, 24% report strong ROI
- 94% pricing page visitors not sales-ready

### Contract/Legal
- 73 days avg delay ($500K+ deals)
- 94 days avg delay ($2M+ deals)
- 23% of "commit" deals collapse in legal
- Contract intelligence → 58% faster legal passage

### Competitive Intelligence
- Stale in 30 days, updated quarterly (10+ week lag)
- 8-12 hrs/month per rep on manual research
- $195K-$292.5K annual cost for 50-person team
- AI coaching → 21% win rate improvement

### Post-Sale Expansion
- 5x more spent on acquisition vs expansion
- Expansion: 50-70% success rate, 80-90% cheaper, 2-3x faster
- <3% upsell acceptance rate common
- NRR 110%+ → premium valuations

## Cross-Cutting Themes
1. **Data quality is the root cause of many downstream problems** — CRM decay feeds into bad forecasts, bad territories, bad intent signals
2. **Time poverty is universal** — SDRs, managers, and CS teams all lack time for their highest-value activities
3. **AI adoption is early and failing** — 81% use AI but 1% mature; 79% of automation fails ROI targets
4. **Personalization vs Scale is the central tension** — quality drops as volume increases across every channel
5. **Handoff points are revenue leaks** — marketing→sales, sales→legal, sales→CS all lose context and speed
